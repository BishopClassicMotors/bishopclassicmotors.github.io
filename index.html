Restoration. Preservation. Legacy.

Bishop Classic Motors is an auto restoration shop and dealership, specializing in European vehicles from 1979 and earlier. We pride ourselves in the exceptional care we provide to clients and the classic vehicles we restore and preserve. Serving both domestic and international markets since 2002, we take vehicles in almost any condition and bring them back to life for later resale.

We maintain a clean, organized, and professional workshop dedicated to our restoration projects, large and small.
